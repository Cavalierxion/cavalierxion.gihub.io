About

Welcome to The Story of the World According to Me , a virtual library, portfolio, and creative space for me to practice my coding skills, display my work, and share some of the things I've been interested in lately.

My name is Christopher Aaron Simpson. I'm a writer and analyst currently living in Austin, Texas with my wife, Sandra, and my cat, Scout.

Officially, I work as a Senior Business Analyst in Client Solutions for UniMed Direct, a software and services company that's working to reduce costs and improve medical outcomes in the worker's compensation industry. I help design, test, and implement our web application, ReviewStat, and show our clients how it can revolutionize their claims process. I spend my spare time writing screenplays, playing board games and learning about the world.
